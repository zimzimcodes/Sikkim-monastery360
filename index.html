Transportation & Travel

Plan your journey to Sikkim's sacred monasteries

Air Travel

Bagdogra Airport (IXB) - 124 km from Gangtok

  • Daily flights from Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata
  • Airport taxi services available
  • Helicopter services to Gangtok

Bus Services

Regular bus services from major cities

  • Siliguri to Gangtok (4-5 hours)
  • Darjeeling to Gangtok (3-4 hours)
  • Local monastery tour buses

Car Rental

Self-drive and chauffeur-driven options

  • 4WD vehicles for mountain roads
  • Local driver guides available
  • Multi-day monastery trail packages
